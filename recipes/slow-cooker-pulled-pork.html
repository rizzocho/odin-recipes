Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Description

I've smoked pork butts in the past. People like it this way just as much, and you don't have to tend a fire.

Ingredients

  • pork shoulder
  • barbecue sauce
  • hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Put pork in a slow cooker, turn on, cook until fork tender.
  2. Remove meat from pot, shred slightly, discarding bits you don't want to eat. Place back into pot
  3. Add sauce heat until ready to eat
  4. Make sandwich, eat sandwich
