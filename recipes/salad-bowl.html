Salad Bowl

Description

Endless customization, and it's healthy.

Ingredients

  • greens
  • grains
  • dressings
  • proteins (optional)
  • toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. Add 1/2 of the base to large bowl
  2. Add 1/2 of the dressing
  3. Repeat
  4. Top with rest of ingredients
  5. Toss, and enjoy
